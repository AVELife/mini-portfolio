Hello Hello! I'm an animation student who just wants to have a good time and look at nice images! I'm currently focusing on storyboarding but I also really like character design.

I really love cityscapes, rococo art, unicorns, goofy music, and video games. (Pokemon BW and Fire Emblem: Awakening being my ultimate favs.)

Outside school stuff, I'm teaching myself how to make ytpmvs and to get better at 3D modelling! I also enjoy collecting figures.

I wanted to use this site as a sort of mini showcase of artworks I've made, some are personal things and others school assignments! Feel free to take a look around!
